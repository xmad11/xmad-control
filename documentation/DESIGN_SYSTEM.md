> ## What Changed?
>
> As of 2025-12-25, the design system has been migrated to the **OKLCH color space** for the restaurant platform.
>
> ### Key Changes:
>
> | Old (RGB) | New (OKLCH) |
> |-----------|-------------|
> | `255 107 53` | `oklch(0.65 0.16 45.0)` - Terracotta Coral |
> | `59 130 246` | `oklch(0.58 0.14 250.0)` - Serene Blue |
>
> ### New Features:
>
> - **5 Accent Colors**: rust, sage, teal, berry, honey
> - **Hybrid Responsive**: Component-level + token-level responsive
> - **Eye-Friendly**: No pure black/white, reduced eye strain
